EPTA ONEIROS

Epta Oneiros is a website that aims to provide quality content and updates regarding the 7 members of NCT DREAM, which are MARK, RENJUN, JENO, HAECHAN, JAEMIN, CHENLE, and JISUNG. It aims to spread happiness, hope, and dreams to its targeted audience. It is a safe place solely for NCT DREAM and its fandom, DREAMzens, and also to those who are fond of 7DREAM. Wherein through the website the users can witness NCT DREAM’s journey and diverse discography.

It also serves as a social platform where the users could meet and interact with each other. Epta Oneiros is the combination of the Greek word “epta” and “oneiros” which respectively means “seven” and “dream.”
